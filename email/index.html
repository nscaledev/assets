Email Signature Generator

Enter your details in the form below, and double check the preview window. Click the button to copy the info to clipboard. To add your signature to Gmail, follow steps 1-6.

  1. In Gmail, click on Settings in the top right
  2. Select See all settings
  3. Under Signature, Create new and name it nscale, then paste (e.g. ctl + V) into the textbox
  4. Select nscale as the default for new emails
  5. Select Insert signature before the quoted text in replies
  6. Click Save changes down the bottom
